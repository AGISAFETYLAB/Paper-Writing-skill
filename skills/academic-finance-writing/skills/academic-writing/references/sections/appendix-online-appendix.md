# Finance Appendix And Online Appendix

## Purpose

The appendix supports the main paper. It is not a dumping ground for unprioritized analysis.

## Main-Text Anchor

Every appendix item needs a main-text anchor:

| Appendix item | Main-text anchor | Claim supported | Required because | Status |
|---|---|---|---|---|

Allowed appendix roles:

- proofs and derivations;
- model extensions;
- variable definitions;
- sample construction details;
- extra event windows, factor models, or portfolio sorts;
- robustness tables;
- additional mechanisms or heterogeneity;
- data cleaning and replication details;
- disclosure, data-code, and supplemental materials expected by the target venue.

## Writing Rules

- title each appendix table or figure by the claim it supports;
- state how the appendix result changes or preserves the main interpretation;
- keep long proofs readable with proposition labels that match the main text;
- do not move essential identification or central-result explanation entirely out of the main paper;
- remove appendix material that has no claim, no threat, and no main-text anchor.
