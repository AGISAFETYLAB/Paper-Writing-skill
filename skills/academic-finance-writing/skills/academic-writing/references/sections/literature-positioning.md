# Finance Literature Positioning

## Purpose

The literature section explains the paper's location in an active finance debate. It is not a
coverage exercise.

## Closest-Paper Role Map

Build a closest-paper role map before drafting:

| Cited work | Role | What it establishes | Remaining gap | How this paper moves the debate |
|---|---|---|---|---|

Allowed roles:

- benchmark result;
- contrast case;
- method source;
- data or measurement source;
- mechanism source;
- institutional context;
- theory prediction;
- external-validity boundary.

## Writing Logic

Open each cluster with the debate, not with author names. Then show the reader:

1. what prior papers establish;
2. what remains unresolved;
3. what this paper adds through data, design, model, measurement, mechanism, or setting.

Do not write an annotated bibliography. Do not cite a paper unless the sentence states its role.
Avoid claiming novelty by omission; novelty should follow from the role map.
