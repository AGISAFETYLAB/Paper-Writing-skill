# Comment / Replication / Corrigendum Finance Paper Type

Use this profile for post-publication comments, replies, replication papers, robustness sensitivity
notes, corrigenda, errata, or amendments concerning a published finance/economics article.

## Source Anchors

- Journal of Finance submissions describe Replications and Corrigenda for material sensitivities of
  central results or material errors in published papers:
  https://afajof.org/submissions/
- AEA policy describes comments, replies, errata, and corrigenda for published articles:
  https://www.aeaweb.org/journals/comments
- AER editorial policy describes Comments and Replies and the standard for substantive discussion:
  https://www.aeaweb.org/journals/aer/editorial-policy

## Priority Contract

- Primary core: Target Article And Claim, Replication / Error Evidence.
- Evidence core: Sensitivity, Correction, Reply, or Robustness Analysis.
- Compress first: broad literature context, general criticism, long background, and unsupported
  speculation.
- Core floor: protect exact identification of the target paper, claim being tested/corrected,
  reproduction materials, discrepancy, and correction boundary.

| Order | Candidate section | Budget rule | Section role |
|---|---|---|---|
| Front | Abstract | venue-bound | State target article, disputed or corrected claim, evidence type, and consequence. |
| 1 | Introduction | primary-core | Identify the published article, central claim, why the comment/replication/correction matters, and the result of the check. |
| 2 | Target Claim And Original Design | primary-core | Reconstruct the original question, sample, model/proof, result, and claim under examination. |
| 3 | Replication Materials Or Error Source | primary-core | State data/code/proof materials, reconstruction steps, access limits, or source of the correction. |
| 4 | Replication / Sensitivity / Correction Results | evidence-core | Present reproduction outcome, sensitivity, non-robustness, corrected result, erratum, or reply evidence. |
| 5 | Interpretation And Boundary | evidence-core | Explain what changes and what does not change about the original contribution; avoid overclaiming. |
| 6 | Response / Reply Handling | support | When applicable, summarize author reply, cooperative amendment, or unresolved disagreement. |
| 7 | Conclusion | compress-first | State the correction, replication lesson, or remaining uncertainty. |
| Back | Supplement / Replication Package | venue-bound | Include code-output map, data access notes, extra tables, proof corrections, and correspondence when allowed. |

## Flexible Adjustment Notes

- Do not use this profile for ordinary robustness checks inside a new empirical paper.
- The target article must be named and the claim boundary must be precise.
- Keep tone factual and evidence-bound; comments fail when they read as broad critique without a
  material result.
