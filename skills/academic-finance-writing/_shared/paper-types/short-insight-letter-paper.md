# Short Insight / Letter Finance Paper Type

Use this profile for concise finance manuscripts built around one key insight, short original
result, short letter, or brief high-impact contribution.

## Source Anchors

- Journal of Finance: Insights and Perspectives states that Insights articles convey a key insight
  succinctly and sets a short-paper submission route:
  https://afajof.org/jfip-submissions/
- Elsevier financial-economics listings describe finance journals that welcome full-length and
  short-letter papers:
  https://shop.elsevier.com/journals/subjects/social-sciences-and-humanities/economics-and-finance/financial-economics/general-financial-markets
- CRIBFB finance/accounting guidelines list Short Communication Paper as a manuscript type:
  https://www.cribfb.com/journal/index.php/ijfb/AuthorGuide

Take absolute length from the active venue, template, or version-target card. Allocate the active

## Priority Contract

- Primary core: Insight And Design / Model.
- Evidence core: Compact Evidence / Result.
- Compress first: literature review, long institutional background, multiple robustness families,
  and extended discussion.
- Core floor: protect the single insight, identification/model object, and decisive display or
  proof before adding secondary material.

| Order | Candidate section | Budget rule | Section role |
|---|---|---|---|
| Front | Abstract | venue-bound | State the single finance insight, object, evidence/model, and implication. |
| 1 | Introduction | primary-core | Present the question, key insight, why it matters, and how the short paper proves or documents it. |
| 2 | Context And Closest Literature | compress-first | Name only the context and closest competing explanation needed to understand the contribution. |
| 3 | Data / Model / Design | primary-core | Provide the minimal data construction, empirical design, or model setup needed to make the result interpretable. |
| 4 | Main Result | evidence-core | Present the central estimate, proposition, figure, or table and explain uncertainty or formal logic. |
| 5 | Robustness Or Alternative Explanation Check | evidence/support | Include only the checks needed to protect the single insight; move extras to appendix if allowed. |
| 6 | Implication / Conclusion | compress-first | State the economic implication and unresolved boundary without broad speculation. |
| Back | Appendix / Supplement | venue-bound | Put extended data definitions, proofs, or robustness details only when the route allows it. |

## Flexible Adjustment Notes

- If the paper needs multiple mechanisms, long data construction, or several result families, use
  `empirical-research-paper.md` or `theoretical-model-paper.md` instead.
- Keep the contribution singular. A short insight paper fails when it tries to be a full-length
  article in miniature.
- Follow the target short-paper venue's exact word, exhibit, and appendix rules.
