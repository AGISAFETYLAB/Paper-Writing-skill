# CARE Case Report Card

Structured item slots: `items/care.json`.

Use for case reports and small case series centered on unusual presentation, diagnosis, treatment,
or adverse events.

## Required Evidence Slots

- patient information and clinical findings
- timeline
- diagnostic assessment and differential diagnosis
- therapeutic intervention
- follow-up and outcomes
- patient perspective when available
- informed consent and de-identification

## Expected Display Items

Patient timeline, diagnostic workup table, intervention/follow-up summary.

## Common Blockers

Do not overgeneralize from one patient. Avoid patient-identifying details unless consent and
de-identification are confirmed.
