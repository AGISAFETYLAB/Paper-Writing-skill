# SPIRIT Protocol Card

Structured item slots: `items/spirit-2025.json`.

Use for clinical trial protocols before results are available.

## Required Evidence Slots

- scientific rationale and objectives
- trial design, setting, eligibility, recruitment
- interventions and comparator
- outcomes and schedule of assessments
- sample size plan
- randomization, allocation concealment, blinding
- data collection, monitoring, and management
- statistical analysis plan
- ethics, consent, trial registration, dissemination

## Writing Rule

Do not report results. Write future-tense protocol commitments and mark unresolved design choices.
