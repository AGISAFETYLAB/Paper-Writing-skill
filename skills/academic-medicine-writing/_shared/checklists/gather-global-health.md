# GATHER Global Health Estimate Card

Structured item slots: `items/gather.json`.

Use for global health estimates, burden-of-disease analyses, forecasts, and public-health modeling.

## Required Evidence Slots

- health question and policy relevance
- data source inventory and inclusion/exclusion criteria
- data processing and harmonization
- model assumptions and covariates
- uncertainty estimation and propagation
- geography, age, sex, and time stratification
- reproducibility materials and data access

## Expected Display Items

Data-source inventory, map or stratified trend plot, uncertainty-interval table, sensitivity analysis.

## Common Blockers

Do not hide model assumptions or uncertainty when presenting burden or forecast claims.
