# CHEERS Health Economics Card

Structured item slots: `items/cheers-2022.json`.

Use for cost-effectiveness, cost-utility, budget impact, and decision-model evaluations.

## Required Evidence Slots

- decision problem and target population
- perspective, comparators, setting, time horizon, discount rate
- outcome valuation and cost/resource estimation
- model structure, assumptions, and inputs
- uncertainty and sensitivity analyses
- incremental costs/effects and interpretation
- funding, conflicts, and data/model availability

## Expected Display Items

Model schematic, cost/effect table, incremental cost-effectiveness table, uncertainty/sensitivity
plots.

## Common Blockers

Do not present a cost-effectiveness conclusion without perspective, comparator, time horizon, and
uncertainty analysis.
