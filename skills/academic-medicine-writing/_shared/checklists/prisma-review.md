# PRISMA Review Card

Structured item slots: `items/prisma-2020.json`.

Use for systematic reviews, meta-analyses, and closely related evidence syntheses.

## Required Evidence Slots

- protocol registration and eligibility criteria
- databases, search dates, full search strategy
- selection process, data extraction, reviewers
- risk-of-bias assessment method
- synthesis method, effect measure, heterogeneity handling
- study selection counts and PRISMA flow
- included study characteristics
- risk-of-bias results and certainty/limitations

## Expected Display Items

PRISMA flow diagram, study characteristics table, risk-of-bias table, forest plot when meta-analysis
is performed.

## Common Blockers

Do not imply systematic coverage when search sources, dates, or inclusion criteria are absent.
