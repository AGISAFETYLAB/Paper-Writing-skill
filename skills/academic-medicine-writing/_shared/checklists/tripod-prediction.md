# TRIPOD / TRIPOD-AI Prediction Model Card

Structured item slots: `items/tripod.json`.

Use for clinical prediction models, risk scores, prognostic models, and clinical AI models.

## Required Evidence Slots

- intended use and decision context
- data source, study population, inclusion/exclusion
- outcome definition and prediction horizon
- candidate predictors and missing-data handling
- model development procedure and tuning
- internal validation and external validation status
- discrimination, calibration, clinical utility
- deployment constraints and fairness/subgroup issues when relevant

## Expected Display Items

Model pipeline, discrimination/calibration table, calibration plot, external validation table,
decision curve when clinical utility is claimed.

## Common Blockers

Do not claim deployment readiness from internal validation alone.
