# Medicine Venue And Statement Planning

Confirm target-journal instructions before submission-ready wording.

Plan early:

- ethics / IRB / consent statement
- trial registration and protocol availability when applicable
- data availability and controlled-access wording
- competing interests and funding
- reporting checklist when applicable, such as CONSORT, STROBE, PRISMA, TRIPOD, or STARD
- supplementary files and source data

Do not invent approval numbers, registration IDs, accession numbers, or checklist compliance.

## Common Venue Families

| Venue family | What to verify |
|---|---|
| General medical journals | article type, structured abstract, word limit, tables/figures, ICMJE-style statements |
| High-impact clinical journals | trial registration, protocol/SAP, completed checklist, data sharing, safety reporting |
| Public health journals | population definition, ethics/data access, policy relevance, equity/subgroup reporting |
| Nature-family biomedical journals | reporting summary, data availability, source data, competing interests, methods depth |

Use the target journal's current author instructions as the authority. This skill only prepares the
checklist and statement plan.
