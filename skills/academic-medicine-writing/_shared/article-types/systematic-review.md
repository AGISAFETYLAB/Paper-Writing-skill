# Systematic Review / Meta-analysis Profile

Use this profile for systematic reviews, meta-analyses, living reviews, evidence maps, or structured
evidence syntheses.

## Profile Boundary

This profile owns section structure and proportional budget only. Apply
`_shared/article-types/profile-boundary.md` for hard-default and deviation rules.

## Length Budget Source

Take absolute length from the active `target_length_budget`. Protect search, eligibility, screening,
risk-of-bias, synthesis, and certainty details before compressing narrative context.

## Checklist / Study-Type Pairing

Pair with PRISMA 2020 and protocol/extension guidance when relevant.

## Priority Contract

- Primary core: Methods.
- Evidence core: Results and synthesis.
- Compress first: broad background, long narrative repetition of each study, and secondary subgroup
  detail that can move to supplement.
- Core floor: protocol/registration, eligibility, search sources, screening, extraction, risk of
  bias, synthesis methods, and certainty/risk interpretation cannot be omitted.

## Section And Proportional-Budget Reference

| Order | Candidate section | Share of main text | Section role |
|---|---:|---:|---|
| Front | Title / structured abstract | outside main budget unless target counts it | Identify the review question, data sources, synthesis type, main finding, and certainty. |
| 1 | Introduction | 8-12% | Motivate why the evidence question matters and what uncertainty remains. |
| 2 | Methods | 30-38% | Describe protocol/registration, eligibility, information sources, search strategy, selection, extraction, risk of bias, effect measures, synthesis, heterogeneity, and certainty. |
| 3 | Results | 34-42% | Present PRISMA flow, study characteristics, risk of bias, qualitative synthesis, meta-analysis results, heterogeneity, subgroup/sensitivity analyses, and certainty. |
| 4 | Discussion | 16-22% | Interpret the evidence, limitations of studies and review process, clinical/policy implications, and research gaps. |
| 5 | Conclusions | 1-3% | State the evidence-bounded conclusion. |
| Back | PRISMA checklist / search strategy / supplement | outside main text unless target counts it | Carry full search strings, extraction tables, excluded studies, and additional analyses. |

## Flexible Adjustment Notes

- Use `review-perspective.md` for narrative reviews that did not use systematic methods.
- A PRISMA flow diagram and study-characteristics/risk-of-bias display are usually load-bearing.
- Do not claim certainty beyond the risk-of-bias and heterogeneity evidence.
